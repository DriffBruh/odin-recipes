Lasagna recipe

Description

Making lasagna that tastes this good will take time, but it is so worth it!

We'll provide you a step-by-step guide on how to make the lasgna dish

Ingredients

  • Meat - sweet Italian sausage and lean groud beef
  • Onion - you'll need an onion which is going to be cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor
  • Garlic - you'll need two cloves of garlic, which are going to be cook with the meat to add flavor
  • Tomato products - you'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar - two tablespoons of white sugar will do the job
  • Spices and seasonings - fresh parslez, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles - use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses - parmesan, mozarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Lazer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
