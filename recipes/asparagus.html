Roasted Asparagus with Parmesan

Description

Parmesan asparagus is a delicious spring side dish that's easy to make with fresh asparagus spears seasoned with garlic and topped with shredded Parmesan cheese. Roast the asparagus until tender, crisp, and cheesy!

Even the pickiest eaters will love this simple and satisfying Parmesan asparagus recipe. Best of all, it comes together quickly with just five easy-to-find ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Asparagus
  • Parmesan
  • Sea Salt
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Spray the dish with olive oil
  3. Sprinkle asparagus with Parmesan cheese, sea salt, and garlic powder
  4. Roast in preheated oven until fork easily punctures thickest part of stem, about 12 minutes
